Solution: Saintly Professions
Answer: VOWTOBRITAIN

Written by Sam Lam, with ideas from Dawson Do

Each of the strings consist of the names of two Catholic saints (as the title indicates) with their letters interleaved. The first X letters of one name appear uninterrupted; then, the first X letters of the second name also appear uninterrupted; finally, the rest of the letters of both names alternate. X will be used as an index at the last step.

Each pair of saints shares a common profession of patronage (as the title indicates). Each profession corresponds to one of the Livery Companies of the City of London, almost all of which are titled “Worshipful Company of X” where X is a profession. This is clued in the flavor text with the exact words “worshipful” and “company”, as well as a reference to guilds in “gilded”.

To help in determining the right companies, the clues have been given in order so that the names of the professions according to how they are written in the name of the company (such as “Chartered Secretaries and Administrators” instead of just “secretaries”) are all in alphabetical order.
